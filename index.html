NIF

The NIF (tax number) is the first document you need when moving to Portugal.

GET YOUR NIF

The NIF (Número de Identificação Fiscal) is your Portuguese tax identification number, and it’s essential for everyday tasks like renting accommodation, opening a bank account or get a public transport free card.

Price: 50€

ORDER YOUR NIF NOW

REQUIREMENTS

You will need just 2 documents:

  • A valid identification document
    • For EU: National ID Card
    • For Non-EU: Passport
  • Proof of address.

    • There are additional requirements for minors and those who were born in Portugal or hold Portuguese citizenship

GET A TAX REPRESENTATIVE

ONLY FOR NON EU/EEA CITIZENS

Getting a tax representative in Portugal is a requirement for non-EU/EEA Citizen, in order to obtain a Portuguese NIF.

The tax representative is an intermediary who will handle your tax obligations and communicate with the Portuguese tax authorities (Autoridade Tributária) on your behalf.

The price includes requesting the NIF and appointing a tax representative.

PRICE: 100 €

WHY SHOULD I REQUEST A NIF?

  • ATTEND A LOCAL UNIVERSITY

    Some Portuguese universities require a NIF for Erasmus students to complete their enrollment.

  • GETTING A FREE SUB-23 PUBLIC TRANSPORT CARD

    If you're under 23 years old, you can get a free public transport pass…but only with a valid NIF.

  • SIGN A RENTAL CONTRACT

    Protect yourself from scams! A valid rental contract must be registered with the tax office and linked to your NIF.

  • OPEN A BANK ACCOUNT

    A NIF is mandatory to open and operate a Portuguese bank account.

  • SIGN A MOBILE PHONE CONTRACT

    Getting a Portuguese number? You'll need a NIF to sign a mobile phone plan.
