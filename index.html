You will need just 2 documents:

Get a tax representative

Getting a tax representative in Portugal is a requirement for non EU/EEA Citizen, in order to obtain a Portuguese NIF.

The tax representative is an intermediary who will handle your tax obligations and communicate with the Portuguese tax authorities (Autoridade Tributária) on your behalf.

Which Documents Do I Need

You will need just 2 documents:

  • Your NIF number (If you dont have it yet twe can request it for you)
  • A valid identification document.
    • For Non-Eu Citizens: Passport.

As a student. Why do I need a tax representative?

If you are a non-Eurpoean Union (EU) or European Economic Area (EEA) citizen and you want to obtain a NIF (Número de Identificação Fiscal) in Portugal, you are required to appoint a tax representative.
