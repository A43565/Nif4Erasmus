Ready to take your first step towards moving to Portugal?

It all starts with getting your NIF, a must-have for anyone looking to live, work, study or invest in the country.

Which Documents Do I Need

You will need just 2 documents:

  • A valid identification document
    • For EU: National ID Card
    • For Non-EU: Passport
  • Proof of address.

As a student. Why should I request a NIF?

ATTEND A LOCAL UNIVERSITY

Some portuguese universities require that erasmus students have a NIF in order to be enrolled.

GETTING A FREE SUB-23 PUBLIC TRANSPORT CARD

If you are under 23 years old, you are entitled to a free public transport pass, but to make the request you need to have a NIF.

SIGN A RENTAL CONTRACT

Avoid scams! Your rental agreement must be registered with the tax authority and linked to your NIF to be valid.

SIGN A MOBILE PHONE CONTRACT

To have a portuguese number you will need to signa phone contract and give your NIF.

OPEN A BANK ACCOUNT

To have a portuguese bank account you will need a NIF.

As a student. Why do I need a tax representative?

If you are a non-Eurpoean Union (EU) or European Economic Area (EEA) citizen and you want to obtain a NIF (Número de Identificação Fiscal) in Portugal, you are required to appoint a tax representative.
