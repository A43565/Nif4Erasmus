About Us

At NIF4Erasmus, we're dedicated to simplifying the process of obtaining a Portuguese Tax Identification Number for international students and visitors.

Founded in 2022 by former Erasmus students who experienced the bureaucratic challenges firsthand, our team understands the frustration that comes with navigating Portuguese administration as a foreigner.

We've built a streamlined, fully online service that eliminates the need for in-person visits, Portuguese addresses, or complicated paperwork. Our goal is to make your transition to Portugal as smooth as possible.

What Motivates Us

We believe every student deserves to focus on their educational journey without administrative barriers getting in the way.

Having experienced the challenges of moving to Portugal ourselves, we're passionate about creating solutions that make life easier for international students and visitors.

Our team is motivated by the satisfaction of helping thousands of students successfully obtain their NIF and start their Portuguese adventure with confidence.

Why Choose Us

100% Online Process

Our service is completely remote. No need to visit any Portuguese office or coordinate appointments. Get your NIF from anywhere in the world with just a few clicks.

Fast & Reliable Service

We process your application quickly and efficiently. Our standard service delivers your NIF within 2-3 business days, and our express service can get it to you within 24 hours on business days.
